Computer Science learning festival

We’re working with Tablet Academy to deliver a series of Computer Science learning festivals across the UK. These will be designed to cover different areas of the computing curriculum. From identifying parts of a PC, to putting them together to build a PC, right through to your first foray into coding and how that journey can progress.

Each festival day is split into morning and afternoon sessions (mornings include a 15 minute break so they’re slightly longer). As a teacher, you can bring up to five students to experience the full range of tasks at the festival. See first-hand how technology could assist your student’s learning.

Cyber Security learning festival

We’re working with Tablet Academy and Microsoft to deliver a series of Cyber Security learning festivals across the UK. These festivals will help students understand the dangers of being online and how to stay safe, with an intro to the Internet of Things (IoT). All powered by Windows 10 devices.

Each festival day is split into morning and afternoon sessions (mornings include a 15 minute break so they’re slightly longer). As a teacher, you can bring up to five students to experience the full range of tasks at the festival. See first-hand how technology could assist your student’s learning.
